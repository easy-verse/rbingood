Solution

How rbingood works

  1. Pin on the map — anyone can mark a spot that needs cleaning.
  2. Sponsor funds the cleanup.
  3. Participants sign up and come.
  4. Cleanup lasts ~2 hours.
  5. Result is recorded (photos + GPS).
  6. Participants receive a reward*, sponsor gets an ESG pack (PDF/Excel/GPS/CO2 for CSRD).

rbingood is not a one-off event. It's a repeatable process.

Process

2 hours. Simple. Structured.

  1. Briefing (10 min) — what to do, how to sort.
  2. Equipment — gloves, bags, grabbers.
  3. Team cleanup (1.5 h) — we clean together.
  4. Counting — weighing, before/after photos.
  5. Socializing — drinks, networking.

FAQ

Questions?

For participants

Do participants get paid?

Yes. A reward for each sponsored cleanup*. Payment within 3-5 business days to your card or MB Way.

Is it safe?

Yes. Each cleanup has a coordinator, pre-start briefing, and professional equipment.

Is experience needed?

No. 90% of participants come for the first time. We explain everything on-site.

Can I sponsor as an individual?

Yes. You can sponsor the project or a specific cleanup.
